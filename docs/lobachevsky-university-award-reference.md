# Lobachevsky University - Achievement Summary

## Event

XVI Regional Natural Science Conference "School of Young Researcher" in Nizhny Novgorod.
The conference brought together school students presenting research and applied projects across science and technical disciplines.

## My Role

I participated as a project presenter, prepared and defended my work before the expert jury, and competed in my track.

## Result

I became a winner of the conference and took **1st place in the "Applied Informatics and Mathematics" section**.
Special prizes from Lobachevsky University were awarded to selected works, including mine.

Original wording from the publication:
`"Специальными призами Университета Лобачевского были отмечены работы Паршина Даниила, занявшего 1-е место в секции «Прикладная информатика и математика»"`.
